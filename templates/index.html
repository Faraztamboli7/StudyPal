Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our premium service

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will apply at your next billing cycle. Any prorated differences will be credited to your account.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 14-day free trial for all our plans with no credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 support via live chat, email, and phone for all plans. Higher-tier plans include priority support with faster response times and dedicated account managers.
